My Favorite Hobbies

I love skiing and try to hit the slopes as often as I can. I usually ski at the local mountains in Wisconsin, but my favorite place I’ve ever skied is Brundage Mountain in Idaho, which is pictured. The terrain and scenery there were incredible, and it was an unforgettable experience. Whether it’s a local hill or a bigger mountain out west, I’m always excited to get out on the snow!

I love running, whether it’s racing or just hitting the trails around Madison. Last year, I ran two half marathons, and my personal best is 1:29:31. Running is a great way for me to challenge myself, clear my mind, and enjoy the outdoors. Madison’s trails make for some of my favorite runs, especially when the weather is nice! I’m always looking to improve which ties back to personal growth.
