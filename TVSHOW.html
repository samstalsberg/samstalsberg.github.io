About the Show

Dumb and Dumber is a comedy film starring Jim Carrey and Jeff Daniels as two well-meaning but incredibly dim-witted friends, Lloyd Christmas and Harry Dunne. After Lloyd falls for a woman who accidentally leaves a briefcase at the airport, the duo embarks on a cross-country road trip from Rhode Island to Aspen, Colorado, to return it—unaware that it actually contains ransom money for a kidnapping. Along the way, their clueless antics lead to a series of ridiculous misadventures.

Director:

Why I Love This Show

I love Dumb and Dumber because it reminds me of my family. My family and I watched this movie growing up and it is an instant classic. The movie is super quotable and has a lot of memorable scenes that make me laugh every time I watch it. To this day, my family still references this movie a lot and I will never get tired of watching it.
