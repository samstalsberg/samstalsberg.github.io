My Favorite TV Show: True Detective
About the Show

True Detective Season 1 follows detectives Rust Cohle and Marty Hart as they investigate a complex and disturbing murder in rural Louisiana. Spanning 17 years, the story unfolds through present-day interrogations that reveal the dark secrets of the case and the detectives' own personal struggles. With haunting cinematography, a gripping mystery, and deep philosophical themes, the season is widely regarded as one of the best crime dramas ever made.

Director:

Why I Love This Show

I love True Detective season 1 because it is so well made and thought out. There is so much depth to the story and the acting is so good. The first time I watched I was blown away, I had never seen anything like it. The show is so dark and gritty, but it is so well done. I love the characters and the story. I have watched it multiple times and I still love it.
